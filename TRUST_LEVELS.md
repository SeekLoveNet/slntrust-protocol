# SLNTrust — Trust Levels

This document describes the trust levels in SLNTrust and how they should be interpreted.

---

## 🟢 High Trust — Green Badge

This profile has demonstrated consistent activity and has a fully completed presence on the platform.[cite: 1] It reflects a user who has earned confidence through sustained, natural usage.[cite: 1]

## 🟡 Medium Trust — Yellow Badge

The normal starting point for most real users.[cite: 1] This profile is active and shows no negative signals, but is still building up history on the platform.[cite: 1]

## 🔴 Low Trust — Red Badge

This profile may be incomplete (e.g., missing a photo or core details) or has shown very low activity.[cite: 1] It signals a need for caution while the account is being established or reviewed.[cite: 1]

## 🔵 Administrator — Blue Badge

This account belongs to the SeekLoveNet team for support and moderation.[cite: 1] It is always distinguished from regular user profiles.[cite: 1]
